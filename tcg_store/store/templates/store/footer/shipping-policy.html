Shipping Policy

Delivery Policy

That's right, We Deliver Nationwide! No matter where you are located in South Africa, you can safely purchase from TCG Store directly on our website.

Have you placed an order? What's next?

Please wait for an email with shipping information. This will only be sent when your order is dispatched.

Your purchase will be processed as soon as possible, during our regular business hours. Monday to Friday 9am to 5pm.

Orders are dispatched Monday to Friday and usually take 2 to 3 working days to be dispatched.

Average delivery times once an order is dispatched:

  • JHB: 3 to 4 working days
  • Cape Town: 1 to 2 working days
  • KZN: 3 to 4 working days
  • Eastern Cape: 3 to 4 working days

Free Delivery

TCG Store offers FREE delivery on orders over R999.

Orders over R999 entitles the customer to 1x Free Delivery.

  • Free Delivery: Includes 1x shipping interval. The order ships when all in-stock & pre-order products in your cart have arrived.
  • Standard: Includes 1x shipping interval. The order ships when all in-stock & pre-order products in your cart have arrived.
  • Extra Shipping: Orders can ship in a maximum of 2 intervals if your cart contains products arriving at different times.

Once an order is dispatched by TCG Store, it is the responsibility of the customer to make themselves available to receive the order. TCG Store is not responsible for any issues caused as a result of unsuccessful deliveries or instances where the customer has not been available to receive their order. Where a customer has not been available to receive their order, it is the duty of the customer to contact the courier company to arrange an alternate delivery time.

Please Note:

  • Pre-order arrival dates are subject to change and often do. This is often due to production or shipping delays beyond our control.
  • Prices on pre-orders are subject to change when the items arrive and can be amended before orders are dispatched. In order to secure a pre-order, the payment in full is required. This payment is non-refundable.

Can you send some of what I ordered early?

Should you wish to split part of an order to be sent ahead of time, it will require the purchase of an additional R99 shipping charge if the items being sent do not meet the R999 Free Shipping threshold.

Once your order has been dispatched, you will be provided with a tracking number whereby you can track and trace the progress of your order.

Quantity & Order Adjustments

We reserve the right to adjust quantities of products ordered. These decisions will be made on a case-by-case basis, and you will be notified of any changes.

Delivery Rates

TCG Store offers 1x free delivery on orders over R999. Orders with a total value less than R999 will carry an additional charge of R99 for delivery.
